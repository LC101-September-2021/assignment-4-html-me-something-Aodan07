    About us

  • Jennifer

    • "Jennifer grew up in Texas and every time they visited their cousins
    they would take a small walk down to Dairy Land and pick up one of their
    quesadilla burgers. Since they did not leave there they would dream of the
    day the where able to return. Until one day when Jen said "lets make our own"
    and so began the hunt to make the perfect quesadilla burger.
    After years of hard work and a lot of eating the Burger was finally completed!"



  • Craig
